Dancing is so much more than just grooving on the dance floor to your favorite tunes.

In fact, you’ll be surprised how many benefits are associated with dancing. Not only does it train your brain and your body’s motor skills but it also is an excellent exercise for your entire body.

As such, it helps you to stay physically and mentally fit.

Even more so, dancing regularly can improve your general well-being, boosts your self-esteem and has also been shown to improve your social skills.
